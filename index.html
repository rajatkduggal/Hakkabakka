The Story

Established with passion, Hakkabakka is an eclectic cafe adorned with vibrant artwork, rustic charm, and an atmosphere that feels like home. Our diverse events schedule includes Live Music Nights, Acoustic Sessions, and themed evenings that keep the energy alive for our guests.

"Hakkabakka is the perfect blend of entertainment and cuisine, with live performances, a diverse menu, and an ambiance that stays with you long after you leave."
— Founder, Hakkabakka
